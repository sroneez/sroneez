I'm **Shofiqur Rahman Soyon** — a Junior Flutter Developer at **[Spark Tech Agency](https://sparktechagency.com)**, currently pursuing a **BSc in Mathematics** at National University (Expected Graduation: 2028).

My foundation in mathematics isn't accidental — it shapes how I think about software. I approach every codebase the way I'd approach a proof: methodically, precisely, and with a relentless focus on structure. That mindset drives my passion for **Clean Architecture**, where complexity is tamed, codebases scale gracefully, and every layer has a reason to exist.

Right now, my world revolves around:

- 🐦 **Flutter** — crafting cross-platform mobile experiences that feel native and polished

I believe great software is a blend of mathematical precision and human empathy. I'm always learning, always building.
