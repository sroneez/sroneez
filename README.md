## 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm **Shofiqur Rahman Soyon** — a Flutter Developer at **Spark Tech Agency** in Dhaka, Bangladesh.

I build cross-platform mobile applications that are engineered to last. My craft is defined by a deep commitment to **Clean Architecture** — not because it's fashionable, but because I've seen firsthand what happens when structure is an afterthought. Every layer has a purpose. Every abstraction earns its place.

Here's what drives me day-to-day:

- 🐦 **Flutter & Dart** — delivering pixel-perfect, performant apps across iOS and Android from a single codebase
- 🏛️ **Clean Architecture + GetX** — writing code that future-me (and future teammates) will actually appreciate
- 🧩 **Data Structures & Algorithms** — grinding problems in **C++** and **Dart** to stay sharp at the fundamentals
- 🌙 **Purposeful Software** — from religious education platforms to social apps, I care deeply about what I build and why

I take freelance projects seriously. When you hire me, you get structured thinking, clean deliverables, and a developer who treats your product like his own.
